IN | Ariba Testing Business Analyst

As an Ariba Testing Business Analyst, your responsibilities revolve around ensuring the quality and functionality of Ariba's procurement and supply chain management software solutions. This involves analyzing business requirements, developing comprehensive test plans and strategies, and creating detailed test cases covering various testing aspects. You execute these test cases manually or through automated tools, meticulously tracking and reporting defects. Collaboration with stakeholders is vital, as you communicate testing progress, issues, and risks, working closely with developers and product managers to address any identified issues. Maintaining accurate documentation of testing artifacts is crucial for reference and audit purposes. Additionally, you continuously strive for process improvement, staying abreast of industry trends and best practices. During user acceptance testing phases, you provide support and guidance to users, ensuring their feedback is appropriately addressed. Ultimately, your role plays a critical part in upholding the quality, reliability, and performance of Ariba's software solutions, contributing to overall customer satisfaction and business success.
