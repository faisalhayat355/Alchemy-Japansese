  • Internet of Things

With expertise in integrated physical and digital design, rapid prototyping and digital platform engineering services, EPAM offers a unique, end-to-end approach to your Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Medical Things (IoMT) and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) ecosystem. By seamlessly identifying the right concept, developing the underlying technology and building and testing prototypes, our comprehensive IoT expertise enables you to deliver innovative solutions and business models that enhance customer experiences in accelerated time-to-market.

Our Core Focus Areas

EPAM’s holistic approach to IoT ensures you can effectively design, implement and optimize solutions that drive value for your business: 

DEVICE MANAGEMENT

We implement, integrate and optimize experience-led secure IoT device setup, from hardware to software design.

PERSONALIZATION

We use our AI and ML capabilities to glean insights from data and create smart IoT devices capable of making highly personalized, real-time recommendations.

BIG DATA UTILIZATION

We build and optimize analytics platforms that seamlessly integrate with IoT devices – collecting and analyzing data streams to help you make more informed business decisions.

CLOUD ORCHESTRATION

We optimize cloud-enabled IoT solutions to reduce technical debt, enable continuous software delivery and improve time-to-value for your organization.

RAPID PROTOTYPING

Through our Made Real Lab, we focus on rapid iteration, combining advanced IoT prototyping technologies, such as mechanical, electrical and robotics engineering, together with AR/VR experimentation tools to ensure your IoT solutions are made for real consumers.

EXPERIENCE DESIGN & CONSULTING

We weave creative thinking and opportunity identification into our design and consulting process to develop human-centered IoT devices and software that provide the best possible customer experiences and pave the way for future innovation in the IoT space. 

INTEGRATED PHYSICAL & DIGITAL DESIGN

Our integrated approach to physical and digital design and digital platform engineering enables us to identify the right concept, develop the underlying technology, build and test prototypes, and accelerate your time-to-market.
