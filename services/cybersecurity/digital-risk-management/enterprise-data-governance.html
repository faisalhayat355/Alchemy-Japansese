Enterprise Data Governance

As more consumer privacy and data protection regulations emerge, your business must have the right digital solutions in place to manage compliance now and in the future.

CCPA IS HERE

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is now in effect and you need to ensure that your organization is complaint. It takes time to build and implement the right solutions and processes to avoid costly pitfalls and disruptions to business operations.

The right combination of enterprise data governance solutions allows you to proactively respond to regulations and reduce costs with compliance, risk assessment and control.

LESSONS LEARNED FROM GDPR

Regulations affect the entire organization, not just those responsible for compliance. It’s critical to act as soon as possible so that business operations go on, uninterrupted. Here’s what we’ve learned by helping more than 20 clients implement enterprise data governance solutions for GDPR:

START NOW

CCPA will have a huge impact on your digital ecosystem and can take 6-9 months to implement processes and solutions. Planning sooner than later will help you decrease costs and response time in the long-run.

Get support from key stakeholders

Changes in business operations due to new regulations involve cross-department cooperation. You need key stakeholders to ensure that internal teams are working together to execute your DRM strategy effectively.

Build a team & choose the right tools

Working closely with a trusted technology services partner will enable you to implement processes and solutions that will not only help manage CCPA compliance, but also to be flexible and agile enough to respond to changes in requirements and new regulations.
