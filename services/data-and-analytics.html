Data & Analytics

Building a future-ready enterprise with the power of data

Contact Us

Navigating AI disruption requires a robust data foundation.

We’re in the midst of an AI revolution. To embrace AI and take advantage of what these new technologies have to offer, it’s crucial to have a robust data foundation in place so you can deliver value for your business. With deep expertise in data and analytics, business intelligence and cloud platform development, EPAM can help you navigate the complexities of AI and build and scale new data capabilities that are required in this new environment. From migrating your data platforms to the cloud to implementing data governance practices across your enterprise, we help you unlock powerful data-driven outcomes for your business so you are prepared for whatever the future may bring and can execute at scale.

Our Core Focus Areas

Data, Analytics & AI strategy

Data, Analytics & AI strategy

Our integrated team of business and technology experts work with you to define your north star vision aligned with your business strategy, assess the current state of your data ecosystem, build a roadmap to deliver data and AI solutions to market and bring that vision to life to maximize value. Using our proprietary methodologies and best practices we've learned from hundreds of successful client engagements, we help drive your data-driven enterprise into the future.

Data Factory & Data Mesh

Data Factory & Data Mesh

We help you build a solid data foundation, offering a unique framework that helps you share, access and manage analytical products in complex and large-scale environments. Our data factory approach is empowered by the data mesh concept and solidified by strong data governance practices so your enterprise can unlock the value buried in your data silos and deliver tangible business results. 

Data Governance

Data Governance

We help enterprises along their data management journey — from the beginning when data governance is most crucial for accurate, accessible, usable data through to the delivery of mature data catalog solutions. We cover the full spectrum of data governance organizational and process changes including master data management, data literacy, data quality, data catalog implementations and data security. This helps ensure you have a sustainable and reliable foundation for your data.

Data Migration

Data Migration

We support you throughout the complex process of cloud data migration from choosing the right migration approach and strategy to leveraging our automation tools, adopting cloud-native services and standardizing the process.  By using proper goal setting and continuous cost optimization processes, we help you mitigate the risk of program failure and enhance overall efficiency. With EPAM, migration is not just a shift but a step towards a modern, cost-efficient and scalable system.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Using our pragmatic, engineering-driven approach, we help you cut through the AI buzz to deliver compelling products that unlock value for your business. From advisory services and organizational change management through cutting-edge AI platform delivery and ML/LLMOps, we offer end-to-end services so you can gain and retain a competitive advantage with AI.

Business Intelligence & Self-Service
