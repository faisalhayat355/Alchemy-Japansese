2020 Corporate 
Responsibility Milestones

As a technology company firmly integrated into the dozens of cities where our offices are located, we have always put our communities first. 

That means using our deep technological expertise to design solutions to strengthen our communities and share our knowledge with the next generation. Read on to learn more about how we helped and get to know those who went above and beyond to create a better world.

Supporting Our Global Community

COVID-19 has profoundly impacted the world around us, presenting us all with an opportunity to mobilize, act for good, give back and help fight the pandemic—#TogetherAsOne.

As a global company, we recognized the need to act with urgency. We made significant steps to ensure the safety and well-being of more than 40,000 EPAMers and our continued operations for hundreds of clients around the world. We also strengthened our commitment to assist our global and local communities—taking proactive measures, donating resources and developing open source contributions to support relief efforts in the communities where we live and work.

Leading The Next Generation Of Technologies

We have a responsibility not just to share our talents and knowledge through our work with clients, but to help empower young creators and coders to become the tech leaders of the future. Through education programs, EPAMers get kids excited to learn about technology, create accessible spaces where students from anywhere can collaborate and create, and partner with experts to make a difference in the lives of students everywhere.
