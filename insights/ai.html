AI Everything: The Next Major Disruption is Here

For decades, artificial intelligence (AI) has blended into the background of our lives — mostly to automate the mundane, tedious tasks that we dread to do. Today, AI has grown to be much more, well, intelligent. For the past six months, generative AI specifically has consumed headlines with its ability to create digital art, pass the bar exam and more, stoking fear and intrigue among the masses.

Its potential is enormous. And its acceleration is being fueled by tech giants, industry leaders, research institutions and the like. Early adoption rates have exceeded those of any disruptive technology to date, outpacing the smartphone, the ATM and the internet. Some tech companies have already developed their own commercialized versions of this tool, making the market ripe for one of the biggest technology disruptions we’ve ever seen.

As with any disruptive technology that enters the market, companies are now tasked with evaluating the business case for adoption and implementation. There’s no time to wait around and see what happens. If you want to compete on the cutting edge of technology, you need to embrace AI. We’ve been experimenting with AI for decades, exploring the opportunities and challenges that come along with it, and we’re here to share what we’ve uncovered…
