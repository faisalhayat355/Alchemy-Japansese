Why Next-Gen Customer Experiences will be MACH-Powered

SHARE
  • link copied

DATE

May 12, 2021

Brands are accelerating investments in omnichannel customer experience (CX), so the need for agile content and commerce solutions is steadily increasing. To create competitive customer experiences, composable architecture is vital. Microservices, API-driven, cloud and headless (MACH) are some of the fundamental components of composable architecture.

In this webinar, our experts address the biggest misconceptions around MACH technology and discuss how composable architecture influences transformation. 

Watch this webinar to learn: 

  • Analyst views about shifts in the MarTech landscape
  • Why it doesn’t have to be all or nothing when it comes to your MarTech ecosystem
  • How to boost CX with composable architecture and avoid falling behind due to common misconceptions
  • The different approaches to composable architecture 
  • How to refine your business case and get started quickly
