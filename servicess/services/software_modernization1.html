Software Modernization


Alchemy Techsol’s is a Software Modernization Hub, where we usher your software into a new era of efficiency, agility, and innovation. Our tailored solutions redefine legacy systems, transforming them into dynamic assets that drive your business forward.

Key Focus Areas

Monolith to Microservices

Monolith to Microservices

    Experience a seamless transition from monolithic structures to a modular Microservices architecture. Our iterative approach, coupled with Restful API generation and framework upgrades, ensures agility and scalability.

Iterative Modernization

Iterative Modernization

    Digital transformation is a continuous journey. We monitor, identify, and rectify architectural issues, reducing technical debt and adapting to evolving architectures with every sprint. Our approach fine-tunes domain boundaries and develops modernization tasks for ongoing improvements.

Refactor Monoliths into Microservices

Refactor Monoliths into Microservices

    Witness the evolution of monoliths into efficient microservices. We extract new microservice code, establish RESTful APIs, remove unused elements, and enhance frameworks. Using a dedicated refactoring studio, we automate class refactoring and perform impact analyses for a streamlined deployment.

Continuous Transformation

Continuous Transformation

    Digital transformation is not a one-time project but an ongoing process. Our strategy prioritizes incremental modernization, technical debt remediation, agile integration of architect-driven stories, and user-prioritized microservice extraction, aligning with evolving business needs.

Modernization as a Continual Service Improvement (CSI) Activity

  • Our process begins with a thorough analysis of your existing architecture. We evaluate your current systems to identify both opportunities for improvement and any potential challenges.
    Analyze the Architecture
  • Addressing identified issues is a crucial step in the modernization journey. We work to fix any architectural shortcomings and establish a baseline for improvement.
    Fix Issues & Baseline
  • Continuous monitoring is essential to ensure the sustainability of the modernized architecture. We vigilantly observe for any instances of architectural drift, keeping a watchful eye on emerging issues.
    Observe Architecture Drift
  • Communication is key. As we observe and identify changes in the architecture, our insights are communicated to your architects.
    Inform Architect
  • Upon identifying issues and informing architects, we take proactive steps to remediate and resolve any challenges. This involves implementing strategic solutions to address architectural drift, reduce technical debt, and ensure continuous improvement.
    Remediate and Resolve
